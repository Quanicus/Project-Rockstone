Mission

This one man crew is captained by the regarded Quan "The Pants" Pham. On a self-proclaimed pilgrimage to plunder the ponderous powers present within his peers. Blinded by the light he finds his way, hiding in the darkness keeps his fears at bay. Reach for the stars, grasping at straws, when will he fall? To those that paved the way he pays homage, because from them he gained great knowledge.
