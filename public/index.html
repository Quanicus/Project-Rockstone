MISSION

This one man crew is captained by the regarded Quan "The Pants" Pham. On a self-proclaimed pilgrimage to plunder the ponderous powers present within his peers. Blinded by the light he finds his way, hiding in the darkness keeps his fears at bay. Reach for the stars, grasping at straws, when will he fall? To those that paved the way he pays homage, because from them he gained great knowledge.

The ReconStar Initiative

It is known that intersteller trade within the entirety of our current traversible star cluster is largely dominated by Scamazon's Jimmothy Bezinos and his gang of inter-galactic goons. Now it seems that everyone wants a piece as they latch onto Scamazon's affiliate program to peddle their mass-produced gagets. When Scamazon was first established, its competetive pricing was the major appeal to consumers. However, today's shoppers crave the convenience of next-day delivery using their proprietary Prime-drive warp technology. A large percentage of the population don't even bother to shop around for more competetive pricing and reflexively smash the dopamine inducing 'buy now' button. The Phantom Protocol was implemented by Capt. Quan for the purpose of obtaining valuable intel of competing retailers' pricing.
